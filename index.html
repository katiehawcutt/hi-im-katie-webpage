Hi, I'm Katie...

School of Code Bootcamper 4.0 // Musician

about.

Let's start with the basics...

I'm 31, I live in Birmingham, I play the saxophone, I love music, art and design, I'm a plant mom, I love the outdoors, travelling, climbing mountains and wild swimming, I enjoy baking, gardening and cycling, I love food (cooking and eating), and more recently (and excitingly) I have come to love coding!

Then...

I’ve spent the last 10 years working in the music industry as a professional saxophonist & tutor which has been fantastic! Since graduating from Leeds Conservatoire in 2010 with a BA(Hons)Jazz, I have worked with a number of different jazz, rock & pop bands playing at festivals, small venues and big theatres all over the UK. As well as gigging, I have also worked with some fantastic schools, music services and arts organisations to provide inspirational music education to young people and adults through ensembles, workshops and outreach programmes. It's been wonderful but recently I've felt like I want to get stuck into something new so I'm embracing a new challenge in life and learning to code!

Now...

Since I started learning to code earlier this year I’ve really taken to both the technical and creative aspects of programming. I've been learning HTML, CSS & JavaScript and I've loved the challenge of getting to grips with these new languages and implementing my new-found knowledge to create some really cool projects and apps. To document my progress I've been doing #100DaysOfCode and you can follow my journey on Twitter or check out my diary on Github. A few months ago I decided I wanted to take the plunge and work towards making it my career and I'm super proud to be able to say that I'm now a School of Code Bootcamper 4.0 and a full-stack developer in training! Languages and topics covered on the bootcamp are HTML, CSS, Advanced Javascript, Node.js, API's, DB's, React and Cloud Hosting, with a focus on project work and team work. By the end of the course, I will have learned the fundamentals of programming, know how to work in a high performance tech team, and I will be specialised in full-stack JavaScript development.

I've made this website as part of my pre-bootcamp homework and I've restricted myself to the use of HTML & CSS as per the instructions!

I'm currently working on my portfolio (so please watch this space!) but you can see some of the projects I've been working on so far on Github.
